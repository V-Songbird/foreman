---
id: 097
title: Resolve upstream decision docs at direct parents only, not transitively
date: 2026-07-23
---

## Decision
`cmdNextCandidates` emits `depends_on_docs` per candidate by resolving each
candidate's **direct** `depends_on` ids to their `doc` values (dropping
`"none"`/empty), not by walking the dependency chain transitively. The roadmap
skill lists those paths in the handoff's `background`/`context` when the field
is non-empty. `craft-prompt` is left untouched.

## Context
Entry 097 asked for the field but deliberately left two questions open: whether
to walk `depends_on` transitively, and whether `craft-prompt` needs the same
treatment. A decision was needed before shipping the field.

## Alternatives rejected
- Transitive walk of the dependency chain — a grandparent's decision reaches
  the task only through its parent's own decision doc, which already cites it
  via `supersedes`; walking transitively would flood the handoff with docs the
  parent already subsumes.
- Treating `craft-prompt` too — it assembles no candidate from roadmap fields,
  so it has no `depends_on` to resolve; nothing to change without inventing a
  new input.

## Consequences
Flipping `decisionLog.enabled` back to true (the entry 097 unblocks) can now
rely on dispatch-time doc surfacing in addition to the file-triggered
`hooks/decision-anchors.js` path. Future work that wants grandparent docs must
add the transitive walk explicitly — the emitter stops at direct parents by
design.
